About Us

Who We Are

Our goal is to make sure that every guy that wakes up and decides he wants a great haircut can get one at The Shave. That day! We are committed to providing exceptional quality, friendly customer service and an awesome experience unlike any other shop.

At GQ Barbershop, we are dedicated to providing affordably priced, unparalleled hair styling and grooming services that are tailored to every client and their lifestyle in a distinctively unique atmosphere. As customers are our number one priority, we value every client and their individual needs.

Our Barbershop offers both a style and community experience as a true "neighborhood shop." Our social setting provides our customers with an opportunity to relax and enjoy their surroundings while experiencing a customized level of service. Our barbers and stylists pair modern day cutting edge grooming techniques with timeless traditional services, which proves in fact.

Depending on the Day.
Monday-Wednesday are by Appointment only. Thursday-Saturday are walk-in days.

Only Barber and person in the chair are allowed in the room at one time. Walk-ins are informed to return at an approximate time.

We will make it right! We will try our best to make you look your best. Whether that is pairing you with another barber or setting you up for a free cut next time, we will make it right!
